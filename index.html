Major Heritage Sites of India

Taj Mahal

Built by Emperor Shah Jahan, the Taj Mahal is a symbol of eternal love and Mughal architectural excellence. It is recognized as one of the Seven Wonders of the World.

Qutub Minar

The Qutub Minar, standing tall at 73 meters, reflects early Indo-Islamic architecture and marks the beginning of Muslim rule in India.

Hampi

Once the capital of the Vijayanagara Empire, Hampi is renowned for its stone temples, royal complexes, and rich cultural legacy.

Ajanta Caves

The Ajanta Caves contain ancient Buddhist art, paintings, and sculptures that date back to the 2nd century BCE.
